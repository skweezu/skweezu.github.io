Hi, my name is Nyk and I'm a years old artist. I've been sharing my creative pursuits online under various names across many places for more than a decade. In 2018, I started making weird edits using a handful of Android apps (primarily Snapseed and Glitch Lab, with a helping hand from GIMP occasionally) and began posting them to Instagram, which is now my primary creative outlet. I roam a lot creatively, though; writing, vector tracing, photography, handcrafts, web and logo design... Lots of novelty-seeking.

Everything I do is free to you. Save it, share it, use it for whatever you want; please credit me, if appropriate. Don't monetize it. FYI I create NSFW content sometimes. You can find most things on my Mega drive.
