Everything Is

I was listening to "Talking About Money" by Wye Oak over and over again when I wrote this. I was obsessed with how Jen ends that song with two unfinished sentences, with how they speak volumes without needing to be said. I was also thinking a lot about love, my relationship, and life.
The mind wanders, and then I wondered. I wondered and wondered and wondered and wondered and wondered and

Then a door opened. I knew it was a trap but it promised anything. Can you? Should I? Have we? Uncertainty is guaranteed.

    Maybe it's anything.
      But what if?

          Stop asking

        Everything is
